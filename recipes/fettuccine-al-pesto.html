Fettuccine Al Pesto

Description

Basic pesto made with basil, olive oil, pine nuts, and Parmesan cheese. This recipe differs from the classic preparation by the addition of parsley. You can add a little extra Parmesan to the mixture if you like.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Basil
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Garlic Cloves
  • Pine Nuts
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Pulse the pine nuts and olive oil until they're finely chopped.
  2. Add the basil leaves and pulse again.
  3. Add the parmesan cheese and pulse again.
  4. Add the pasta water (or optionally heavy cream) and pulse until the desired consistency.
